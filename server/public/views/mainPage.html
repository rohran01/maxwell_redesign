I grew up in Wautoma, Wisconsin, a town of 2,000 people. I spent my early years reading books, singing songs, painting pictures, and watching movies.

I left home and ventured to the magical land of Minneapolis to pursue a career in art. Four years later, I graduated from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design with a BFA in Filmmaking. While in school, I produced a feature-length musical and started designing at 3M full-time. After my contract ended, I found myself in the Creative Services department of FOX9, doing all the things I love.

I now live in St Paul with my dog, Chewie. In my free time I enjoy reading books, singing songs, painting pictures, and watching movies.

I guess some things never change.

Creative Producer

I worked both on my own and with others in the department to concept and execute on-air and social media promotions. This included writing, shooting, lighting, editing, animating, and coordinating with talent. I've been the recipient of four Upper Midwest Regional Emmy's, including a Promotion: News - Single Spot for my Embezzler promo. As a designer, I created logos, print/web graphics, and signage for various station events. I was also an integral part of FOX9's re-branding efforts, where I developed a new logo and visual look that was clean, classy, and contemporary.

Creative Producer

I was a key designer for The Jason Show, creating the logo, in-show animations, and promotions for on-air and web.

Undone is a new, feature-length musical - Written and Directed by Maxwell Freudenthal, alongside Composer Nic Delcambre. The film made its public debut at the OUT Twin Cities Film Festival in Minneapolis, Minnesota on June 3rd, 2012 - winning the Festival’s coveted “Best Minnesota-Made Film” Award. Written, cast, directed, and produced almost entirely in Minnesota - Undone represents the passion of Minnesota-based artists and professionals who came together and worked tirelessly to produce an incredible piece of cinema.
